Once upon a time, there was a little girl from a small, tiny, unknown little town in the deep mountains of Ayacucho. You're helping your mother run errands around the town, a common duty you've had your entire life. The Sun starts slowly setting when mom suggest it is time to start heading home. Constantly reminding you to watch your step, more specifically "dont slip". You see the light from the only lamp post in the entire town shining as you walk down. WHen suddenly you feel it, the rocks under you slide across your feet and you feel yourself fall down, as if you were just tripped. The fall itself wasnt bad, but it was definitely weird, almost as if something grabbed you. Your mom turns around, concerned... all she says is "no te puedes dormir." (You cannot go to sleep). Confused, you ignore the commnent, assuming your mom was just trying to scare you. The night falls, you finish dinner when you begin to yawn. Your mom looks at you again and says, "trata de no dormir, en la mañana regresamos a las montañas para pedir disculpa" (try not to sleep, tomrorowing we will return to the mountain to apologize.)
